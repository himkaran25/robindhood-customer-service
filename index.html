How to Reach Robinhood Support: A Complete Guide

Having issues with your Robinhood account? Whether it's related to account access, trading problems, or technical glitches, it's important to know the different ways you can contact Robinhood Support. Here's a breakdown of the most effective ways to get help.

1. In-App Support (Preferred Method)

Robinhood encourages users to contact support through the mobile app or website. Follow these steps:

  • Open the Robinhood app.
  • Tap the Account icon (person icon) in the bottom-right corner.
  • Select Help.
  • Tap Contact Us.
  • Choose the topic you're having trouble with and follow the prompts to request support.

2. Request a Call

You can request a phone call from Robinhood through the app or website:

  • Follow the same steps as above until you reach the support topic.
  • Look for the option to request a phone call.
  • Robinhood will typically call you back within a few minutes.

3. Email Support

Although not the fastest method, you can send an email through their website:

Visit the Robinhood Contact Page and fill out the appropriate form for your issue.

4. Help Center

Robinhood's Help Center has hundreds of articles and answers to common questions. It’s the best place to start if you want fast answers without waiting for support.

5. Social Media (Not for Account Help)

Robinhood maintains official profiles on platforms like Twitter (@RobinhoodApp) and Facebook. While they do respond to general inquiries, they won’t help with account-specific issues for privacy reasons.

6. No Public Phone Number

Robinhood does not offer a direct phone number for inbound calls. All phone support must be requested through the app or website.

Final Tips

  • Make sure your app is up to date before contacting support.
  • Provide accurate information and screenshots (if applicable) to speed up the process.
  • Be cautious of scams—Robinhood will never ask for your password or verification codes via phone or email.

If you're locked out or experiencing a time-sensitive issue, contact them through the app immediately and request a callback.

We hope this guide helps you reach Robinhood support quickly and easily.
